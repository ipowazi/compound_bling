Housing

I think the housing landscape will change as we run out of oil and gas prices increase, water becomes scarce in certain areas, the population ages, and the Fed's purchases of fixed income securities to keep interest rates at historic lows tapers off. I am very interested in keeping up to date on trends and researching cash flow positive properties in neighborhoods that will thrive in the future economic landscape. The Doctor Housing Bubble Blog is my favorite.

Astronomy

We live in a universe that is potentially infinite and may contain other earthlike planets with earthlike humans or unearthlike planets with full-blown aliens. The closest solar system is light years away, so it is unlikely that we will ever figure out all the mysteries of the universe. Humans in their current form have only been around for 50,000 years. If the human race is to survive the coming supernova of our sun (which is really far away), we will need to move to other planets. I don't know much about astronomy, but the topic facinates me.
